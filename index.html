About Us

We are a team of ecologists, foresters, and GIS specialists dedicated to delivering scientifically credible carbon sequestration and biodiversity solutions to industries across India. Our consultancy supports compliance, ESG goals, and environmental stewardship.

Our Services

  • Carbon Sequestration Studies for Industrial Greenbelts
  • Biodiversity Assessment and Monitoring
  • Custom Greenbelt Plantation Design
  • ESG Reporting Format Preparation
  • Power BI Training for ESG Data Visualization

Methodology

We apply field-proven methods including plant census, biomass estimation, ecological surveys, and GIS mapping to assess carbon potential and biodiversity in industrial greenbelts. Reports are tailored to BRSR, GRI, and CDP standards.

Industries We Serve

  • Steel & Foundry
  • Pharma & Chemicals
  • Textile & Manufacturing
  • Special Economic Zones (SEZs)
